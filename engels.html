Wingsuit flying is a skydiving sport where you glide through the air in a special suit. This special suit is called a ‘wingsuit’. The wingsuit generates increased lift, which lets you stay longer in the air. Also you are gliding through the air instead of just falling down which happens when you skydive. You end your flight with opening your parachute, the same as being used for parachuting and base jumping. The max speed is 400 kilometers per hour.

Location: differs per event and the time of the year
Price: op request
Gear: on location


Skydiving is jumping out of a flying plane. When you jumped out of the plane you start with a free fall. After that, you open your parachute and you fly to your destination.
