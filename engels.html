About us

Stegmann events is an event agency that is specialized in the organization of hobby and extreme sports related events for private and business/corporate customers. Stegmann events takes over the whole organization of an event. Which immediately offers many benefits like:

- Time saving: these days not everyone has the time to organize an event themselves. The advantage of an event agency is that has all the knowhow and experience with the organization of an event. That saves you a lot of time in which you usually already need to find out all the options.

- Money saving: as you are saving time, you automatically save money. We organize the event effective and efficient.

- Risk reduction: Stegmann events is known with all aspects of an event. Think about the emotions that are evoked, visitors, materials. Next to that Stegmann events also takes care of safety measurements and insurances if needed.

To sum up Stegmann events takes care of activities, location, entertainment, catering, etc.

A few examples of events that Stegmann events is able to organize are:
- A online race grand prix.
- A footbal match in a footbal stadium.
- Mountainbiking through the mountains.
- And we also have skydiving for the people who like that.

With Freeride MTB you are going to push your boundaries. You drive over anything you are able to drive over. Next to that you learn how to jump with your MTB. Wingsuit flying is a skydiving sport where you glide through the air in a special suit. This special suit is called a ‘wingsuit’. The wingsuit generates increased lift, which lets you stay longer in the air. Also you are gliding through the air instead of just falling down which happens when you skydive. You end your flight with opening your parachute, the same as being used for parachuting and base jumping. The max speed is 400 kilometer per hour.
